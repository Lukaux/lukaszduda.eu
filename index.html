Few words about my skillset

Research

I design and run continuous quantitative and qualitative research for products with a multi-million user base. I combine surveys, behavioral data, desk research, interviews and large-scale feedback analysis to uncover user problems and unmet needs. I translate insights into clear, actionable inputs for design and development, supporting confident product decisions.

Design

I translate research insights into clear information architecture, UI design, and coherent user experiences. I design user flows and wireframes, and craft UX writing that supports clarity, accessibility and ease of use. Working closely with product and engineering teams, I simplify complex processes and reduce user friction.

Strategy

I support product development through data-driven, user-centric decision-making. I analyze competitors, market trends and regulations, then provide new directions for development. I help teams prioritize initiatives through research, workshops and clear frameworks. My goal is to align user needs with business goals.

Cross-disciplinary skills

I use behavioral economics to better understand user decisions and support more effective product choices. I'm highly proficient in Excel and have extensive experience with AI, which I use to organize and analyze data. I continuously learn new industry tools and practices, enjoy working closely with cross-functional teams and take full ownership of the tasks I'm responsible for.
